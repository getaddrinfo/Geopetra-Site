Who we are

GeoPetra Limited is a UK based oil and gas services company which provides domain expertise in:

  • oil and gas technology assessment, commercialisation and deployment
  • design and implementation of digital oilfield facilities and programs
  • domain expertise in the specification, implementation and ongoing support of 24/7 real-time drilling solutions
  • well engineering and petrotechnical data management services to Common Data Access (CDA) reporting standards and Oil and Gas UK well abandonment guidelines
